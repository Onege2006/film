Boys Over Flowers

When Geum Jan-di, a working-class girl, earns a scholarship to the prestigious Shinhwa High School, she finds herself caught between privilege and prejudice. There, she meets the F4, a group of wealthy and handsome boys who rule the school. Despite challenges and heartbreaks, Jan-di stands her ground, navigating love, rivalry, and identity in a world built for the elite.
