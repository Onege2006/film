Slovo Patsana

Slovo Patsana. Blood on the Asphalt is a crime drama about coming of age, honor, and the harsh rules of the streets. Set in the 1990s in Kazan, the story follows teenage gangs fighting for influence, living by a code where keeping one's word is everything. A story of friendship, betrayal, violence, and destinies shaped in a ruthless world of lawlessness.
